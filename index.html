Get Started with Trezor: Secure Your Crypto with Trezor Suite

Ready to take control of your cryptocurrency journey? Connecting your Trezor hardware wallet to Trezor Suite is the easiest way to manage your digital assets securely and efficiently. Follow the simple steps below and get started with confidence.

Secure and Simple

Every action within Trezor Suite requires confirmation on your Trezor device, adding an extra layer of protection to keep your funds safe. Whether you’re new to crypto or a seasoned investor, Trezor makes it simple, secure, and straightforward. Start your crypto journey today at Trezor.io/start and discover the world of safe, seamless crypto management.

Step 1: Grab Your Trezor Device


  • The first step is to get your Trezor hardware wallet. Whether you have a Trezor Model One or Trezor Model T, you're just a few steps away from complete control over your crypto. Plug your Trezor device into your computer or mobile device to begin the process.

Step 2: Visit Trezor.io/start


  • Next, head to Trezor.io/start, where you can easily download the Trezor Suite app. This all-in-one software allows you to manage your cryptocurrencies, check your balances, and even make transactions securely. Trezor Suite is designed to be user-friendly, without all the confusing tech jargon—just a simple, clean interface to help you manage your crypto assets.

Step 3: Set Up Your Trezor Wallet


  • Once you’ve installed Trezor Suite, follow the on-screen instructions to set up your wallet. You’ll be guided through a secure setup process, where you can create a wallet and store your recovery phrase. This process is vital for ensuring the safety of your funds, and Trezor makes it easy to follow along.
